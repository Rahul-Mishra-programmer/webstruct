Our Stores

 

Glasnevin Store:
Our first store opened August 2010.  It is situated at 130 Slaney Road, Glasnevin, Dublin 11, where the Slaney road meets the Finglas road opposite the famous Glasnevin Cemetery. The store, with almost 2,000 sq. ft. of shopping space is located in a retail cluster just at the entrance to Dublin Industrial Estate, in front of LIDL and opposite Woodies.

Parking:
We share LIDL’s 200-space car park, so you can always find parking.

Ennis Store:
We opened this store in December 2011 it is situated in the heart of Market Square, Ennis, Co.Clare. There is ample parking located near the store.
