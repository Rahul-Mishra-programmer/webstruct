Sanctuary for Body, Mind and Spirit

We live in stressful times – today’s life is a fast lane, where we crumble under enormous amount of responsibilities and pressures and we end up chasing time, instead of being able to enjoy the moment, take a breath and just BE. No wonder more and more people suffer from what we call – civilization diseases – anxiety, depression, allergies, IBS, migraines, body pains and cancer… Holistic medicine knows that the source of all illnesses lies in our emotional blocks, negative thoughts, disturbed inner balance and spiritual suffering. By eliminating the root of the disease not just the symptoms we are able to heal it completely.

We offer assistance in the process of your healing and self-improvement through natural therapies such as psychotherapy, hypnotherapy, reiki, shiatsu massage, breathing sessions, healing art and ear candling, nutritional analysis and more,  as well as life-enhancing courses, workshops and group sessions in fields of Energy Healing, Vedic Art, meditation, Law of Attraction and more.

Natural therapies work well on its own or complementary to traditional medicine. There are no side effects only a promise of a better health and happier life. Each of us is different and each has their own path to recovery – we promise individual and empathic approach, understanding and full discretion.

In our peaceful sanctuary you can open up, be yourself, feel the life energy and hope overflow your soul. You will find inspiration, gain inner balance and awaken you body self-healing powers. Your health will improve and you will learn how to create a happy, fulfilling life.
