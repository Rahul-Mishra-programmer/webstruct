Salthill Hotel Galway The Salthill Hotel is located overlooking the picturesque Salthill promenade, with panoramic views of Galway Bay and the Clare hills, and within walking distance of Galway City centre. This latest addition to our hotel collection is a stunning hotel in Galway and the perfect choice for your next hotel break in Galway.
 
 
Carlton Hotel Tralee The Carlton Hotel Tralee, formerly Fels Point Hotel, offers guests a haven of style and comfort, surrounded by the beautiful Slieve Mish mountains, the Kerry countryside and panoramic views of Tralee town.
 
 
Tralee Central Hotel The Tralee Central Hotel, formerly the Abbey Gate Hotel Tralee, has established itself as one of the most attractive and elegant 3 Star hotels in the Tralee and Kerry region.
 
 
Carlton Dublin Airport Hotel The Carlton Hotel Dublin Airport is one of the newest hotels at Dublin Airport. The hotel is located beside Dublin Airport and offers free parking and a free airport shuttle to the airport to guests.
 
 
Carlton Hotel Kinsale The Carlton Hotel Kinsale, one of the most popular hotels in Kinsale, is located on 90 acres of mature wooded parkland with panoramic views overlooking the picture perfect Oysterhaven Bay making it the ideal location for a hotel break in Kinsale Ireland.
 
 
Carlton Shearwater Hotel, Galway The Carlton Shearwater Hotel & C Spa is a stunning 4 star hotel located in the gateway town to the West of Ballinasloe Co Galway. This luxurious Galway hotel is easily accessible from all over Ireland due to its location on the main Galway to Dublin road the N6 within easy reach of both Athlone, Co Roscommon and Galway City
 
 
Carlton Atlantic Coast Hotel, Westport Nestled at the foot of Croagh Patrick, the Carlton Atlantic Coast Hotel offers you a luxury location for your hotel break in Westport. This stunning hotel in Westport has been carefully restored from an 18th century Mill retained an old world charm yet offering a four star luxury hotel experience.
 
 
Carlton Hotel Blanchardstown The award winning 4 star Carlton Hotel Blanchardstown, formerly Plaza Hotel in Tyrrelstown, is the latest addition to the Carlton Group and one of the finest luxury hotels in Dublin. This four star Dublin hotel is conveniently located close to the M50, M2 and M3 motorways, making it easily accessible from all major routes across the country.
