How to Find Us

We are located in the heart of the picturesque village of Lucan in West Dublin. The M50 is a 7 minute drive away (exit 7). The city centre is a 20 minute drive away. Driving from M50 on the N4 westbound take exit number 4. At the top of the slip road turn right (signposted Lucan Village). Continue down the hill until you reach a crossroads. You are now in Lucan Village. Turn right at the cross roads and the Lucan Business Centre is on the right hand side. There is ample parking on the left hand side of the road.
