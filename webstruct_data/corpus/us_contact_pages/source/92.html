Visit a Reference Center

Bobst Library has a multidisciplinary reference desk on the first floor of the library where you can start your research, ask questions, and get subject-specific help from librarians. There are two main reference centers within the library, one devoted to the humanities and social sciences (first floor), and the other to business & government information and physical & life sciences (fifth floor).

Ask a Librarian

The Ask a Librarian service is a place to start your research, ask questions, and get subject-specific help from librarians. You can submit questions via e-mail, IM us from one of our pages or by using screen name "AskBobst," or text your questions to 646-265-1342.

Contact a Subject Librarian or Archivist

You may make an appointment to speak with a subject librarian regarding special research needs. Not sure who to ask? Use our consultation form and we will match you with the appropriate librarian. 

Report a Problem with the Library's Facilities

Use this online work request form to report a building issue to be fixed. e.g.: temperature issues, water fountains not working, cleaning a spill or bathroom issue. Note: type "Bobst" in the Building Search box, and select "BOBST LIBRARY (70 Washington Square South)".
