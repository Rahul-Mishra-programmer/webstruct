Belmont Public Library
336 Concord Avenue, Belmont, MA 02478
(617) 489-2000
belmont@minlib.net
Belmont Public Library Homepage
Directions:

From Rt. 2: take Rt. 60 exit toward Belmont. At second light, turn left. Go through town and under stone bridge. Turn left. Library is located on the right approximately one-quarter mile up Concord Ave.
By Public transportation: Take the 74 or 75 bus from Harvard Station -- Get off at the stop after the Belmont High School.
