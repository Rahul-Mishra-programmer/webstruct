Ask a Librarian

Ask a reference librarian for assistance with:

  • Research
  • Locating library materials
  • Book, movie and music recommendations
  • Using library resources and services

By Email
Submit a reference question and receive a reply within two business days from a professional librarian.

By Phone
The Information Center offers quick reference assistance over the phone. Call (312) 747-4300 for assistance. Hours: Monday through Thursday, 9:00 a.m.-9:00 p.m.; Friday and Saturday, 9:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m.; Sunday 1:00 p.m.-5:00 p.m.


eBooks and eAudiobooks Support

Didn’t find answers to your questions in OverDrive Help ? Contact support for assistance.


My CPL Account Assistance

Online instructions are available to help you access your My CPL account , check the status of your account , track library materials on hold and renew items currently checked out. Updating personal information and resolving account problems must be done in person at any Chicago Public Library location .


Suggest a Purchase

Suggest a purchase to be considered by the Chicago Public Library.


Website Comments

Email comments or questions about the website to webmaster@chipublib.org . This account is monitored during business hours Monday through Friday. You will receive a response within two business days. We welcome your feedback.


General Questions

  • Email info@chipublib.org with all other questions or comments. This account is monitored during business hours Monday through Friday. You will receive a response within two business days.
  • Contact administrative staff .

FOIA Request

The Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) is a state law that provides citizens with access to public records. Visit CPL’s FOIA page to request information about the Chicago Public Library or the services offered.
