About

AVSAR is a team of IT outsourcing and business professionals. We have obtained unique experience and built a very strong team of offshore web and software developers and managers. The projects we have been working on required not only remarkable development and technical expertise, but an in-depth understanding of internet marketing, ecommerce and functioning of many industries.

AVSAR provides consulting and IT services to clients globally – as partners to conceptualize and realize technology driven business transformation initiatives. From strategy consulting right through to implementing IT solutions for customers, AVSAR straddles the entire IT space. As a diverse end-to-end IT solutions provider, AVSAR offers a range of expertise aimed at helping customers re-engineer and re-invent their businesses to compete successfully in an ever-changing marketplace.

The prime concern in all our services is to provide software solutions and resources that are helpful to all our clients to do their jobs with a level of excellence that is unmatched in the industry. In direct response to the high failure rates of traditional enterprise software, AVSAR employs unique strategies, products and business practices to deliver unparalleled results.

For example:

  • AVSAR builds industry-specific products “from the ground up,” and focuses exclusively on the unique needs of large companies.
  • AVSAR solutions are deployed using a methodology that yields accelerated time to value and reduced customer risk.
  • AVSAR is the only software company that directly ties its own revenues and 100% of employee incentives to customer success.

We have a successful track record and experience in maintaining stringent quality, intellectual property and meeting timeline demands of our clients. Our emphasis on complete commitment to customers and a clear perspective of nurturing long-term relationships has ensured satisfied clientele in India and Abroad.

We partner with our clients to invest in replicating their IT environments, training our teams, setting up communication & security systems and integrating our development processes with theirs to ensure that the offshore team is an efficient and seamless extension of the client’s development facilities. We also invest in creating a pool of additional resources that can be used to augment the team at any time.

Add to this a system that optimizes employee productivity and charges Clients attractive long-term rates and you can realize what we offer to our clients on a long-term basis.

In India, we can establish and manage your dedicated offshore development center to develop IT solutions, maintain legacy systems, and provide 24-hour, on-call support.

Our pioneering software solutions support, combined with world-class Offshoring methodologies, enable us to offer services in the areas of Consulting, Solution Integration and Professional Services.

Our commitment to Quality has resulted in our clients relying on our competencies to provide a wide spectrum of value-added services delivered beyond expectations. AVSAR’S Quality Process defines product as well as process measurements that are used to monitor the quality of the products delivered and for the continuous improvement of processes respectively.

Working with our company is very easy and convenient. Our corporate portal was especially developed to provide clients with unique capability to manage the working process on their projects. Our customers have all the tools needed to track development progress, get the information on hours spent and directly communicate with all the project team members.
