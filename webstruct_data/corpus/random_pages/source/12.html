IntegraSpec XL products - NIR Spectroscopy for general process applications
The IntegraSpec XL addresses many NIR PAT applications in monitoring vapor, liquids, and solids in transmission or diffuse reflection and a wide range of general process applications such as chemical/petrochemical manufacturing. The flexible platform accommodates both fiber-to-detector and direct-to-detector sampling configurations. The IntegraSpec XL products also include a comprehensive set of documentation.
