Samenvatting van Greg Steer

By night I may be found in the various guises of photographer, writer, cook, gamer or brewer of tea beverages but by day I am a Microsoft and CompTIA Certified IT Systems Engineer, currently enjoying the Canuck lifestyle in Vancouver.

According to my father I typed my name in to a BBC Micro B at aged four and I haven’t looked back since, progressing through forcing Dune II to run in himem, organizing squads/playing in the Quake II Savage CTF Leagues and leading forty-man raids in the World of Warcraft.

I am a hands-on and enthusiastic IT engineer, well versed in technical hardware and software planning, implementation and support, covering a wide range of systems, with a decade of commercial experience in the UK and Canada and nineteen years of personal computer experience. I am also a skilled problem solver who demonstrates well-honed organisational skills and an on-going dedication to the IT industry through continued self-development.

It rains, it shines and the IT jigsaw puzzles keep requiring inventive solutions, usually resolved over a cuppa tea and a rich tea biscuit.

Specialities:

Microsoft Windows Server consultations, implementations and support from Server 2000 through Server 2012 (inc. SBS variants) and Microsoft Exchange 2000 through 2010 in small to medium businesses.

Office 365 Cloud setup and migrations.

Managed Services management, client taken on and support.
