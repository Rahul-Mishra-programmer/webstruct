  Reflective Material
Foil I S D G

Available in bright and dull silver, and standard colors.  Can be laminated to
paper or one or two sides of C2S board.

Mylar I S D G

Polyester film, more "mirror-like" than foil.  Can be laminated to one or two
sides of board.  Standard and special color matches available. Metalized polyester available in patterns. Also standard and special color matches.

Holographic Polyester Patterns I S D G

Produced on polyester film that is metalized to generate high reflectance and color.  Laminated to solid bleach board.  Available in standard colors and special color matches, several patterns available.

  Pressure Sensitive Material
Pressure Sensitive Vinyl I O S L LM G

Flexible vinyl coated with a pressure sensitive adhesive on a release liner.  Used for bumper stickers, fleet markings, and decals. Available with different types of adhesives for special applications.  Can be top coated for
improved ink adhesion.  Available in colors.

Pressure Sensitive Paper & Tag I S LM G

Coated and uncoated paper, CIS tag.  Available in several finishes.  Paper and tag available with different types of adhesives and can be made with a "split" backing sheet.
